So we're optimizing for Instagram profile views. Now the unfortunate or the limited thing is that Meta doesn't give us a followers objective, so we can't optimize for Instagram follows, but the profile visits is the closest thing because we're assuming that if they are clicking over from your ad to go visit your Instagram page, that they're more interested in what you have to say and they're curious about it.
